    1 Oak is one of New York City's most popular venues, boasting an elite crowd and exclusive parties. The club really gets moving at around 1:30-2, full of models, jet-setters, professionals and bohemians alike. The line is long, and the club is packed, which makes it feel that much better if you can skip ahead to a VIP table. 1 Oak boasts a mixed crowd, ranging from international moguls to downtown artists, but is reserved for the elites of any group. DJs spin a variety of music at 1 Oak, like Top 40 and hip-hop, as well as mainstream EDM / dance music. 1 Oak consistently hosts world-renowned DJs, and guests are often treated to surprise performances. Getting into 1 Oak is easiest (still not very easy) early in the night, but it is strongly recommended that groups reserve a private table to make it through the door.
